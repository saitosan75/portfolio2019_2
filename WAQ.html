Waikiki Aquarium T-shirt Design Illustration

Illustration

Waikiki Aquarium | NMA Internship


I interned at Waikiki Aquarium, and their gift shop wanted to make a T-shirt design, and I created this illustration. They wanted a circular design with Hawaiian sea animals, and I tried to create a movement inside of the illustration using where animals are looking, their body curves, and background color. As T-shirt design, this illustration is using too many colors, and it makes the cost of creating and selling the t-shirt more expensive, but I proud of how much I put effort into and how it came out to this, so I put here.
