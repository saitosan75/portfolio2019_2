Jazz Minds Website

UX/UI Design

School Project | Interface Design 2


Jazz Minds is a bar where you can enjoy live music. Many jazz players like to play here, and I was one of them. That is how I found this place. However, the genre that you can hear at Jazz Minds is broad, like funk, R&B and island music. As their open hours, this place is for night time and most likely for adult. One of the goal to design this website was to create that comfortable, but the interactive feeling from the design to make people want to come and have a beautiful night. Another goal was to organize the website thoroughly to make users easy to get information. Their website back then, I had a hard time to find their monthly schedule. This website had to be helpful for customers, workers, and musicians.
