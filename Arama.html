A·RA·MA

Print

School Project | Corprate Identity

  • In this class, we did branding, and I made a brand called A·RA·MA for aroma diffuser and aroma oils. Most of the time, aroma diffuser is for relaxation, but A·RA·MA also provide unique, spunky, and refreshing aromas and it can enjoy with a group of people. Our target audience is from the busy college student to the professional office job employee. It is for women in their early twenties to mid-thirties. The core attribute for my brand is "fun," "Youthful," "Bold," and "Flavorful."

  • My brand needed to be "flavorful," so I had to create many funny, but good smells for at least some people. In this class, we learned one practical skill in Illustrator that how to create a pattern, and using that skill, and I made a pattern with illustrations of those flavorful smell. I used them everywhere using different colors. I wanted to make a package for aroma diffuser using clear acetate, and I had to research how to print on acetate. The hardest part was building a package with acetate. If you fold it, it gets ripped off, so I had to be careful. Also, it is clear, so I had to find a way that the glue does not show up as much as I can, and I found out a glue tape was the best.
