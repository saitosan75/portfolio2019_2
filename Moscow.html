Moscow City Logo

Illustration

School Project | Graphic Symbolism


Moscow is the capital and most populous city of Russia, and the reason why I chose this city was that I was listening to a song called "Moscow." Even I got interested in that city because of that reason though, I searched the city and found out they have a lot of original and historical buildings, designs, and decoration everywhere. The reference for my logo is the straw doll that is burned at the end of their traditional festival called "Maslenitsa." At this festival, people make a wish for warm spring and summer at the end of the winter, and they make a shape of the sun for the dolls head. My logo is from that doll wearing a Russian crown and made it colorful like Saint Basil's Cathedral in Moscow.
