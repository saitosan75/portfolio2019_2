Take a Trip on a Rocket!

Motion Graphic

School Project | Motion Graphic Design


This project was creating a visual essay, and I chose to create an advertisement for the rocket that space X is developing. This rocket allows you to travel to other countries quickly and I found this news when I was searching which theme I was going to do. The background music is from a British children's TV show broadcasted in 1965, called "Thunder Bird." I had watched it when I was small, and I chose this music because I thought it is perfect for this visual essay. To make the animation and music go together, I imported music first, find when the sound comes in exactly through playing over and over, and animated. This is what I put much effort into. What I struggled was the difference in type length for each place. Some frames fit the type perfectly, but not some of them especially "Los Angeles". The solution was to change the type size for overall.
