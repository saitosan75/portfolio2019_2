Conducting

Motion Graphic

School Project | Digital Imaging


In this project, we learned how to make a rotoscope using after effect and photoshop. I did not want to take a video of myself, and when I thought about my boyfriend who currently teaches middle school band. The idea went well because, for players, it is normal to see the conductors facial expression, but for the audience, it is not possible usually. I asked him to wear a shirt, jacket, and pants and recorded a video f him conducting with his favorite piece. However, I forgot to ask him to tuck in his shirt inside of his pants, so I was guessing when I was drawing his waist. When we play music, I kept thinking the attack of the note can be visualized like a splash. If you know a visual and music artist called "Blue Man Group," their paint splash on drums are an excellent example. I wanted to show that powerful feeling of music in this rotoscope.
