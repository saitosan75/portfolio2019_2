Big City Diner

Illustration

School Project | Graphic Symbolism


Big City Diner is a local restaurant which has a long history and famous in Hawaii. The first restaurant opened in 1998, and like their name, restaurants are conceptualized the 20s to the 40s diner. Big City Diner is one of the restaurants that have a more extensive history and opens a bunch of places on Oahu. Current Big City Diner's logo is using a lot of bright colors with gradient and too detailed, so it will be hard to see everything when you make the logo smaller. I tried to keep their strong characteristics and diner feeling in the logo and to show the connection to the keywords "Hawaii," "egg," and "morning."
