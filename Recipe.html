Makoto's Recipe

Print

School Project | Interface Design Studio


This book is a recipe book of my father's restaurant. His restaurant closed the beginning of this year, and I wanted to make something for my father to praise the past 7 years of his work. The restaurant was Japanese food restaurant, and many of the customers were from Japan. The recipe is not supposed to spread around, but to make it able to read anybody here, I wrote this book both in Japanese and English. The hardest thing was to gather the contents and to think about wording for both languages. I got a bunch of memos from employees left at the restaurant, and I was digitalizing them, but some of them are wrong and different from others, so at the end, I had to ask my father. Also, the restaurant was small, so they did not have enough high-resolution pictures. The solution pictures were to use pictures on the internet and add the visual reference, I put my hand-drawing.
