Bouncing Moon

Motion Graphic

School Project | Motion Graphic Design


In this project, we learned how to animate bouncing ball using easy-in and easy-out, and we made 100% original story and style motion graphic utilizing that skill. I was looking for a sphere that can be the main character. When I thought about the moon, I was wondering how the moon can bounce, and this story came up. The most thing that I cared was timing. If it does not right, it becomes awkward. I realized this while I was working on this project.
