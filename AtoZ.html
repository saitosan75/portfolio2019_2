A to Z in Waikiki

Print

School Project | Typography


This project is about finding all alphabet from a particular place and making a photo book of it. I decided to find alphabets of restaurants in Waikiki and creating a perfect binding book. The things that I struggled was taking a good picture and printing. I took photos with my phone, but some letters were far away and couldn't get to close. It was the first time printing many pages on a big paper. Also, I was not used to the school printer, so I had a hard time setting up. However, after many trials, those problems became not a problem at the end of this project and helped me to do my work more smooth after that.
