Sinapple

Print

Artwork | Kirie


This is my hand made artwork made with paper, glue, and black pen. This kind of artwork is called kirie. It is Japanese traditional art. The proper way to make kirie is curring out a black paper in shape and gluing colored paper from underneath. Instead of black paper, I drew on white paper, cut out in shape, and colored the paper into black. After I created this artwork, I put this in a frame and put on the wall on my father's restaurant. Then I saw the color of the colored paper changed after a while because of sunburn. I learned which paper's color can change easily, so next time when I create something like this, I will not use that paper. This artwork eventually vectorized and became a short video as you can see on my homepage.
